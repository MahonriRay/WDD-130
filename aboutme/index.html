About Me

Mahonri "Mo" Ray

Rainbow Six Siege and Apex Legends are the games I play most at the moment, if you're trying to run some games, let me know!

I've only been at BYU-I for roughly a week now, but one of the things I love most about this place might be the beautful gardens or the calm nature of the campus.
