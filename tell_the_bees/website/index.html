Tell The Bees!

For as many beekeepers that are in the world, there are just as many reasons for why these individuals care for bees. Asides from the sweet, sweet honey that you can obtain from such a rewarding task as beekeeping, it is a super humbling experience that teaches patience and how to care for delicate living animals. Whether you’re reasoning to keep bees is for the honey, to add another sort of “pet” to your family, or it’s purely for hobby, I hope that you’ll be able to find guidance in my website as you embark on your journey of beekeeping!


The Bee is domesticated, but not tamed.
